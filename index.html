Welcome to the Home of Ireland's only VW Syncro 4x4 Event!


This website was built to become the main focal point of an annual Irish event for VW Syncro enthusiasts. We aim to promote and encourage engagement from the community across the island of Ireland, the UK, and international visitors.


Our goal is to encourge enthusiasts to sign up to receive a newsletter regarding Syncro (and other 4x4) events, and to build a cohort of volunteers to ensure the event's continued success.


And remember, the invitation is not just for VW Syncro owners, but extends to all other 4x4, off-roading and camper van enthusists. So what are you waiting for? Click the link for our registration form at the top of the page and let's get you signed up!


News about upcoming and past events can be found on our events page.


Please connect with us on our social media platforms at the bottom of the page.
