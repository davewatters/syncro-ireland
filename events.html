Eire Syncro was the first ever Syncro event held in Ireland in 2011


Eire Syncro was the first ever Syncro event held in Ireland. This event was attended by the French Syncro Club and also Club 8090 Syncronauts of which the organisers of Eire Syncro were also members.

From what we know there were approximately 16-20 Syncros in total in the whole of Ireland so it was quite amazing to have our ranks swelled to approximately 30 Syncros with our French and English fellow Syncronauts and some members from the Irish VW clubs.

The event was held over two weekends - the first part in the East of the country, and the second in the West, which gave all who attended a week to go exploring in between. We would very much like to repeat this event and this websote has been built to advertise and promote it so that we can do this again. Please enjoy all the amazing photos and videos that were taken by the participants, including some of my own, and please feel free to comment and share on our social media platforms.
